Healthy Brain Network (HBN)

The HBN study

  • a landmark pediatric mental health study.
  • collecting MRI and clinical assessment data from 10,000 NYC area adolescents
  • includes dMRI
  • openly available on FCP-INDI

Alexander et al. An open resource for transdiagnostic research in pediatric mental health and learning disorders. Sci Data 4, 170181 (2017).

Challenge

  • Data is BIDS-ish, which is a euphemism for not BIDS-compliant.
  • No publicly available preprocessed dMRI.
via GIPHY

Solution

Apply best practices via QSIPrep, perform QC, and host on FCP-INDI.

TLDR: Preprocessing large datasets is hard. Do it once!
